Welcome to Sandwich Express!

We are located in Lincolnshire and we have a delivery radius of 25 miles. We offer bulk lunch deliveries in order to keep your costs low. We have a strict rule that all of our fresh ingredients will be local to Lincolnshire, or if not possible, be grown within 25 miles of Lincolnshire. We aim to cater to as many people's needs as possible, so whether you're a Vegan, Vegetarian, Coeliac, Pescetarian, we will always try to make our menus suited to you.

We aim to maintain a high level of service, buy taking feedback from our customers. We try to make sure that everyone. We also offer bulk sandwich platters, suitable for every occasion.
